# Contributing to RheumFellow.com

Thank you for your interest in contributing.

RheumFellow.com is an open-source rheumatology education framework focused on board-style learning, clinical reasoning, and AI-assisted educational workflows.

## Ways to contribute

You can contribute by:

- Improving documentation
- Suggesting educational templates
- Reviewing sample question structure
- Adding references to publicly available guidelines or educational resources
- Improving clarity, formatting, and accessibility

## Medical content policy

All medical content should be educational only and should not be interpreted as medical advice.

Clinical questions, vignettes, and explanations should be:

- Evidence-based
- Reference-grounded
- Clinician-reviewed when possible
- Free of patient-identifiable information

## Protected content

Please do not submit:

- Patient data
- Protected health information
- Copyrighted board-review content
- Proprietary clinical datasets
- Paid-resource screenshots or copied material
- API keys or credentials

## Commercial use

Educational content in this repository is intended for non-commercial use unless written permission is granted.
